Lerruat Log Resort, Kajiado

Exceptional Scenic Hospitality

Elegantly perched 7,000 feet above sea level, on one of the many sprawling hills within Kumpa in Kajiado, Lerruat Log Resort offers jaw-dropping views unlike any other holiday destination - the ultimate expression of Luxury in the Wild!

Immerse yourself in the serenity and charm of our rustic yet luxurious accommodations, where every detail is designed to elevate your holiday experience. Whether you're seeking a romantic getaway or a family retreat, our resort offers the perfect blend of relaxation and adventure. 

Wake up to the stunning sunrise over the hills, indulge in outdoor & indoor activities, and then unwind by the cozy fireplace or roof top restaurants and discover the magic of our hilltop paradise – your ultimate escape awaits.
